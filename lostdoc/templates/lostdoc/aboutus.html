About Tafuta

Tafuta is a Webbased and Mobile Application aiming to revolutionize document management systems (DMS) and recovery within Africa. Our solution helps solve the tedious processes involved while replacing lost documents by saving both time and money while recovering the documents. Among the documents we handle include but not limited to National IDs, Passports, Driving licences and academic documents.

Our Core Values
  • Confidentiality
  • Integrity
  • Availability

We give the power of content provision to the to upload the lost and found documents and we do promise to ensure that our systems are user friendly and easy to use in the search and upload of the lost documents. Our main focus currently is the National Identification cards issued by NIRA, this we hope will enable citizens to engage in the upcoming elections and also access other public places due to the recovery of their Documents.
